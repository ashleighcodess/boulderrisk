About Us

Built on discipline, leadership, and execution.

Boulder Risk Associates, Inc. is an independent insurance and risk advisory firm built for the underserved commercial middle market—and for the owners and teams whose personal risk is tied to the business.

We work with leaders who treat risk as a financial and operational exposure, not an administrative checkbox. Boulder separates itself through disciplined execution: we challenge the status quo, hold carriers accountable, and stay involved beyond placement through claims advocacy, coverage interpretation, and continuous optimization.

We negotiate from a position of preparation and knowledge. We remain actively involved through policy issuance, initial review, daily service activities, and quarterly review meetings. Boulder Risk Associates Inc. also remains involved in any claims process and continues to ensure outcomes meet expectations.

We are only a phone call away.

We back our communities, put people before money, and partner with businesses that demand precision, ownership, and results—every year.

Our Principles

Core Values

Five commitments that define how we operate—on our best days and our worst.

01

Humans First

We treat people like people—always. On a bad day, we don’t get sharp, sloppy, or dismissive; we protect the relationship and the standard.

02

Humble & Open-Minded

We assume we’re missing something—and find it. We don’t dig in to “be right”; we ask better questions, invite pushback, and adjust fast when facts change.

03

Extreme Ownership

If it’s ours, we own it—no excuses. Close loops, fix problems at the root, and bring solutions, not drama.

04

Give Credit, Take Blame

We elevate others and absorb the heat. We don’t throw anyone under the bus; we handle mistakes directly and immediately.

05

Support Local Community

We build where we live and serve. Local-first decisions, show up consistently, and support causes aligned with our standards.
